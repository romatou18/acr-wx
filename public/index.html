ACR Alpine Weather

NZ alpine forecasts delivered to your Garmin inReach via satellite

Prerequisites

You need a Garmin inReach device with an active satellite subscription and the Garmin Explore app (or explore.garmin.com) linked to it.

Step 1 — Add the bot as a contact

1
Open Garmin Explore

Use the mobile app or visit explore.garmin.com

2
Go to Contacts → Add Contact

Enter name: ACR Alpine Weather

3
Enter the bot email address
wx.acr.apps@gmail.com
4
Save the contact

Step 2 — Send the UPDATE message

1
Compose a new message

Select ACR Alpine Weather as the recipient on your device

2
Type the command
UPDATE

Your device will automatically attach your current GPS coordinates and a session token — the bot uses these to fetch the right forecast and route the reply back to you.

3
Send via satellite

Step 3 — Receive the forecast

Allow up to 2 minutes. The bot polls for new messages every minute, then fetches live data from yr.no, MetService NZ, and the NZ Avalanche Centre.

You will receive 1 or 2 messages (Garmin's 160-char satellite limit):

Message 1 — Day 1
YR T:12C D1:41mm D2:91mm | MS(AOR) D1 Rain w/ heavyFalls S W1k:75 2k:95 3k:95
Message 2 — Day 2 + Avalanche
D2 Shwrs then rain W1k:100 2k:110 3k:120 | AVL:4-HIGH
YR T:
Temperature °C (yr.no)
D1: / D2:
Precipitation mm, day 1 / 2
MS(xxx)
MetService park code
W1k / 2k / 3k
Wind km/h at 1000 / 2000 / 3000 m
AVL:
Avalanche danger 1–5 + rating

All commands

Message textWhat it does
START Begin tracking — weather sent on location updates and twice daily (07:00 & 19:00 NZT)
UPDATE One-off immediate weather fetch for your current location
STOP Stop all automatic updates

Test without a device

Email the bot directly

Send an email to wx.acr.apps@gmail.com — the reply arrives in your inbox within ~2 minutes.

Subject or bodyWhat it does

update Forecast for last known location

HTTP API

Query the forecast directly from any app or browser.

GET /weather-api?lat=-43.73&lon=170.09
GET /weather-api/all
